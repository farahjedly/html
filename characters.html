Characters














Finn

Finn, the main protagonist of Adventure Time, is a courageous and enthusiastic young adventurer with a heart of gold. With his loyal companion Jake the Dog, Finn journeys through the colorful and whimsical Land of Ooo

AdventureTime

Adventure Time is a whimsical and imaginative animated series that takes viewers on a surreal journey through the enchanting and unpredictable Land of Ooo.




























Jake

Jake, the lovable and shape-shifting dog in Adventure Time, is Finn's faithful companion and best friend. With his stretchy abilities and laid-back personality, Jake adds humor and wit to their adventures in the Land of Ooo
